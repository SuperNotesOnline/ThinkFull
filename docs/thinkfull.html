Specialist Roles: Expanded

Discipline Deep-Dives

Key roles with expanded responsibilities. Each one brings its own lens to every recommendation.

Experience Engineering

Experience Engineer

Design memorable experiences that go beyond usable to unforgettable.

  • First visit impressions that earn trust immediately
  • Empty, loading, and success states as storytelling moments
  • Onboarding and celebration experiences
  • Ask: What will users remember? What will they share?
Motion & Animation

Motion Designer & Hero Specialist

Create world-class visual experiences benchmarked against the best.

  • Hero animations with full 10-point spec (see table)
  • Scroll storytelling and product reveals
  • Never animate for the sake of animation
  • Motion enhances understanding, not decoration
AI Systems

AI Systems Designer

Design intelligent systems that improve workflows responsibly.

  • Design for uncertainty: AI outputs are probabilistic
  • Give users control and override paths at every AI decision
  • Make reasoning visible when it builds trust; hide when it creates anxiety
  • Instrument AI quality: track accept / edit / reject rates
  • Graceful degradation when AI is slow or wrong
Copy & Content

Copy & Content Strategist

Words are a product surface. Every string must be crafted with intention.

  • Voice and tone consistency across all touchpoints
  • CTA strength and microcopy quality
  • Headline, error message, tooltip, onboarding copy
  • Messaging frameworks and content strategy
Data & Analytics

Data & Analytics Lead

Instrument everything that matters. Define success before building.

  • KPI frameworks and event taxonomy
  • Funnel instrumentation and retention cohort design
  • A/B test architecture
  • Measure user value, not just user activity
Growth & Community

Community & Ecosystem Designer

Products that create network effects outlast products that don't.

  • Community loops that increase value as users grow
  • Creator and partner ecosystem design
  • Viral and referral mechanics built into core product
  • Developer ecosystems and APIs as growth surface
Innovation

Innovation & Future Trends Dir.

Look beyond today's solutions to what's category-defining.

  • What will users expect in 3 years?
  • What will competitors miss?
  • What could redefine this category?
  • Think like an inventor. Think like a visionary.
Conversion

Conversion Experience Architect

Design experiences that naturally guide users toward action.

  • Onboarding completion and activation rate improvements
  • Trust signals and objection handling
  • Decision friction reduction
  • Balance persuasion with user experience
